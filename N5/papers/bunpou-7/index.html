bunpou-7.10 (Mondai 3)
→ [5]番。
  1. 1. ぐらい
  2. 2. しか
  3. 3. だけ
  4. 4. でも ✓
Rationale: 「でも」 attached to a quantity means "even (just)" - 一日でも = "even (just) one day". Combined with 「ぜったいに」, this expresses "I definitely want to play, even just for one day next week." 「ぐらい」 (approximate) would clash with 「ぜったいに」 (firm resolve).
