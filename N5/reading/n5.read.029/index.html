なつ

Topic: weather · Mondai: 4

ENなつ

HIजापान की गर्मियाँ बहुत गरम और उमस-भरी — टोक्यो में 30°C आम; एयर कंडीशन सर्वव्यापी।

Passage

なつは とても あついです。毎日 30どより 高いです。よる、エアコンを つけて ねます。なつ休みに うみに 行きたいです。

Translation (natural)

Summer is very hot — over 30 degrees every day. I sleep with the air conditioner on at night. I want to go to the beach during summer vacation.

Translation (literal)

Summer is very hot. Every day, it is higher than 30 degrees. At night, turning on the air conditioner, I sleep. During summer vacation, I want to go to the sea.

Cultural context

Japanese summers (なつ) are hot and humid, especially in cities like Tokyo where temperatures regularly exceed 30°C. Air conditioning (エアコン) is universal but switching it on overnight is a routine summer-survival habit. The summer break (なつ休み) for students typically runs from late July to late August.
