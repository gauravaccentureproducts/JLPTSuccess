わたしの しゅみ

Topic: hobby · Level: easy · Mondai: 4

ENわたしの しゅみ (hobby विषय का छोटा अनुच्छेद)।

HIलेखक का शौक़ खेल — रोज़ सुबह पार्क में दौड़, शनिवार दोस्तों के साथ फ़ुटबॉल।

Passage

わたしの しゅみは スポーツです。毎日 あさ こうえんで はしります。土曜日は ともだちと サッカーを します。とても たのしいです。

Translation (natural)

My hobby is sports. I run in the park every morning, and on Saturdays I play soccer with my friends. It's a lot of fun.

Translation (literal)

My hobby is sports. Every morning, in the park, I run. On Saturdays, with my friends, I play soccer. It is very fun.

Cultural context

Public 公園 (parks) in Japan are heavily used at dawn for jogging and ラジオ体操 (radio calisthenics — a national morning fitness ritual broadcast since 1928). Saturday soccer with friends is a common adult amateur pattern, often arranged via casual community 草サッカー (kusa-sakkā, "grass-roots soccer") groups.
