えきの ちかくの みせ

Topic: daily · Level: N5 · Mondai: 4

ENえきの ちかくの みせ

HIघर के पास एक नया स्टेशन है — स्टेशन से निकलकर बायें मुड़ने पर एक सस्ती दुकान मिलती है, जहाँ दरवाज़े पर खड़े वृद्ध मालिक हमेशा सज्जन हैं। सड़क के पार एक बड़ी दुकान भी है, महँगी पर तरह-तरह का सामान।

Passage

私の いえの ちかくに、新しい 駅が あります。駅の 出口を 出て、ひだりに あるくと、安い 店が あります。その 店で 入口に 立って いる おじさんは いつも しんせつです。道の むこうにも 大きい 店が あります。そこは 高いですが、いろいろな ものが あります。

Translation (natural)

There's a new station near my house. Take the exit, walk left, and you'll see a cheap shop. The man who stands at the entrance there is always nice. Across the road there's a bigger shop too — pricey, but it has lots of things.

Translation (literal)

Near my house, there is a new station. Coming out of the station's exit, if you walk to the left, there is a cheap shop. The man standing at the entrance of that shop is always kind. Across the road there is also a large shop. It is expensive, but it has various things.

Cultural context

Train stations are local landmarks; nearby shops cluster around them.
