本やの セール

Topic: shopping · Level: info-search · Mondai: 6

EN本やの セール (shopping विषय का छोटा अनुच्छेद)।

HIजापानी पुस्तक-दुकानों पर सेल दुर्लभ — फ़िक्स्ड क़ीमतें (ていかはんばい) सामान्य हैं।

Passage

本やの おしらせ 大セール！ 日にち：八月一日から 八月七日まで 時間：あさ 十時から よる 八時まで 本は ぜんぶ 半分の ねだんです。 日本語の 本も えいごの 本も あります。 ぜひ 来て ください！

Translation (natural)

Bookstore Announcement Huge sale! Dates: August 1-7 Hours: 10 AM-8 PM Everything is 50% off. Japanese and English books. Come by!

Translation (literal)

Bookstore announcement Big sale! Date: from August 1 to August 7 Hours: from 10 in the morning to 8 at night All books are at half price. There are Japanese books and English books. Please come!

Cultural context

セール (sale) at Japanese bookstores is rare — most shops keep fixed prices set by the publisher (定価販売). When sales do happen (e.g. 半分のねだん, "half price"), they're usually stock-clearing events at independent shops, advertised with おしらせ (notice) flyers.
