きょうの てんき

Topic: weather · Mondai: 4

ENきょうの てんき

HIआज बहुत ठंडा — सुबह बारिश हो रही थी, अब नहीं हो रही पर ठंड है; कल बर्फ़बारी की आशंका।

Passage

今日は とても さむいです。あさは あめが ふっていました。今は ふっていませんが、まだ さむいです。あした、ゆきが ふると おもいます。

Translation (natural)

It's really cold today. It rained this morning. It isn't raining anymore, but it's still cold. I think it'll snow tomorrow.

Translation (literal)

Today is very cold. In the morning, rain was falling. Now it isn't falling, but it is still cold. Tomorrow, I think snow will fall.

Cultural context

Japan's climate varies sharply by latitude — Hokkaido and the Sea-of-Japan side get heavy snow (豪雪, gōsetsu), while Tokyo sees light snow only a few times a winter. The 「雪が ふる」 in this passage suggests the speaker lives in a snow-prone region or expects an unusual Tokyo flurry.
