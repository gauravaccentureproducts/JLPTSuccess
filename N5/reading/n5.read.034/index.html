友だちからの てがみ

Topic: family · Level: easy · Mondai: 4

EN友だちからの てがみ (family विषय का छोटा अनुच्छेद)।

HIदोस्त के घर पार्टी (学校の パーティー) — जापान में स्कूल पार्टियाँ छोटी कक्षा-पार्टियाँ हैं।

Passage

やまださんへ おげんきですか。わたしは げんきです。あした 学校で パーティーが あります。三時から 五時までです。先生も 来ます。たのしいですから、来て ください。 たなか

Translation (natural)

Hi Yamada, How are you? I'm doing well. There's a party at school tomorrow from 3 to 5. The teacher is coming too. It'll be fun — please join us! Tanaka

Translation (literal)

To Yamada-san: Are you well? I am well. Tomorrow, at school, there is a party. It is from 3 o'clock to 5 o'clock. The teacher is also coming. Because it will be fun, please come. Tanaka

Cultural context

School parties (学校の パーティー) in Japan are usually small classroom social events, not large dances — typically organized by the homeroom teacher and bringing in light snacks. Inviting someone with a brief written note (this passage's style) is culturally appropriate.
