せんしゅうの 日よう日

Topic: weekend · Mondai: 4

ENせんしゅうの 日よう日

HIपिछले रविवार दोस्तों के साथ समुद्र पर — मौसम बहुत अच्छा, तैराकी की, मछलियाँ देखीं।

Passage

せんしゅうの 日曜日に、ともだちと うみに 行きました。天気は とても よかったです。およぎました。さかなも 見ました。とても たのしかったです。

Translation (natural)

Last Sunday I went to the seaside with a friend. The weather was perfect. We swam and even saw some fish — it was a great day.

Translation (literal)

Last Sunday, with my friend, I went to the sea. The weather was very good. We swam. We also saw fish. It was very fun.

Cultural context

うみに行く (going to the sea / beach) is a summer staple. Japan is an island nation, so most major cities are within 1-2 hours of a beach. Popular destinations from Tokyo: Enoshima, Zushi, Kamakura. From Osaka: Suma. Beach activities are swimming, sunbathing, and BBQ. Beach huts (海の家, umi-no-ie) operate seasonally July-August serving food and shaved ice (かき氷, kakigoori).
